Build & Fly: Drone Workshop

A 2-day hands-on workshop where teams learn drone fundamentals, build their own drone from scratch, and compete in a flying competition — with a chance to win a Drone Flying License worth ₹1,00,000!

Register Now ₹300/person (can form a team of 3-5) · 2 Days

About the Workshop

Join Build & Fly: Drone Workshop for an electrifying 2-day drone workshop designed for students who want to explore the world of drones and aerial technology. Work in a team, build a real drone, fly it — and compete for a chance to earn a Drone Flying License worth ₹1,00,000. Open to students from all colleges.

Workshop Schedule

DAY 1

Learn & Start Building

Morning — Theory Session

  • Drone components and architecture
  • Flight principles and aerodynamics
  • How motors, ESCs & flight controllers work

Afternoon — Build Begins

  • Receive drone components kit (worth ₹1200)
  • Start guided drone assembly with mentors
DAY 2

Finish, Fly & Compete!

Morning — Fine-Tuning

  • Continue drone assembly and calibration
  • Testing and troubleshooting with mentors

Afternoon — Test, Fly & Compete 🏆

  • First flight testing session
  • Drone flying competition among top teams
  • Top 3 flyers win Drone Flying License!
