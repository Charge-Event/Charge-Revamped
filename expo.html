About Project Expo

The Project Expo at CHARGE 2026 provides a platform for students to showcase their innovative engineering projects and technical ideas.

Participants will present their working models, prototypes, or research projects related to electronics, embedded systems, robotics, IoT, and other emerging technologies.

This event encourages creativity, problem solving, and technical presentation skills while allowing participants to gain valuable feedback from judges and industry mentors.
