About Paper Presentation

The Paper Presentation event at CHARGE 2026 provides a platform for students to present technical ideas, research insights, and innovative concepts related to engineering and emerging technologies.

Participants will prepare and present their technical papers in front of judges and an audience. This event encourages critical thinking, research skills, and effective technical communication.

Students can present ideas related to electronics, communication systems, embedded technologies, artificial intelligence, IoT, robotics, and other innovative domains.
