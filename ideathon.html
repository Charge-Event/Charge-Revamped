Ideathon Challenge

Do you have a blueprint that can redefine the tech landscape? THE ELECTRONIX CLUB (TEC) invites you to the Ideathon — a battle of brains focused on theoretical innovation and system architecture.

No hardware? No problem. We’re looking for high-level solutions in VLSI, Smart Communication, and Robotics that tackle real-world bottlenecks through logic and design.

This event emphasizes problem identification, feasibility, and the technical “X-Factor”. Participants will present their ideas before judges who’ll evaluate them based on creativity, impact, and innovation.
