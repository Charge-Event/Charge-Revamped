Ideathon Challenge

The Ideathon organized by The Electronix Club is a platform where creative thinkers, innovators, and problem-solvers come together to present powerful ideas that can shape the future of technology and society.

Participants will brainstorm, develop, and pitch innovative solutions to real-world problems across domains such as electronics, automation, AI, sustainability, healthcare technology, and smart systems.

This event focuses on concept creation, innovation thinking, and problem-solving skills rather than building a complete product. Teams will present their ideas before a panel of judges who will evaluate them based on creativity, feasibility, impact, and presentation clarity.

It is a great opportunity for students to showcase their imagination, explore technological possibilities, and gain exposure to real innovation processes.

  • Possibility of mentorship and guidance for developing your idea further.

  • Chance to transform a simple concept into a potentially impactful project.
