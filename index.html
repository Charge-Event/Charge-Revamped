About CHARGE

CHARGE — a decade-long legacy of innovation, creativity, and excellence. Since 2011, it has been the heartbeat of TEC, Department of ECE, SNIST, inspiring generations of engineers to build, innovate, and lead.

For over 10 years, CHARGE has hosted hundreds of workshops, hackathons, ideathons, and expos. Thousands of students have showcased projects from embedded systems to drones, IoT, VLSI, and PCB designs.

CHARGE is not just an event; it's an ecosystem where young minds learn, compete, and collaborate — transforming ideas into real-world technological marvels.

CHARGE Legacy

Since 2011, CHARGE has empowered thousands of students through innovative learning, competitions, and exhibitions. From small departmental projects to national-level hackathons, CHARGE has been a platform for discovery, creativity, and technological excellence.

Each year, CHARGE attracts bright minds, passionate innovators, and future tech leaders. Its legacy is built on collaboration, mentorship, and transforming ideas into impactful solutions.
