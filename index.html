Welcome to CHARGE REVAMPED 2026

A 3-day national-level technical fest organized by The Electronics Club (TEC), under the Department of ECE, at Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology (SNIST).

About the Event

CHARGE REVAMPED 2026 is a celebration of innovation and technology, uniting young minds and creative thinkers to shape the future of electronics and embedded systems. From coding to circuits, from drones to design — this is where imagination becomes engineering.

Dive into challenges, compete, learn, and collaborate with the brightest engineers from across the nation in an environment filled with energy, innovation, and passion.

Major Attractions

⚙️ Workshop

Hands-on industry-driven session on Embedded Systems — covering IoT hardware, firmware basics, and real-world project demos.

💡 Forge Hackathon

24-hour hardware-based Hackathon focusing on Electronics, IoT, VLSI, PCB Design, and Drones. Compete, innovate, and prototype your vision.

🚀 Ideathon

Pitch your next big idea in Electronics and Drones. Convince mentors and judges with innovation and feasibility.

🧩 Project Expo

Showcase your working projects live at the heart of SNIST. Inspire others with your innovation and teamwork.

📑 Paper Presentation

Present your research, reviews, and futuristic ideas in core ECE, IoT, and Embedded domains to a panel of academicians and experts.

🎮 Games

Relax, compete, and recharge with thrilling technical and fun games. A separate arena for entertainment and creativity.

Prize Pool & Rewards

Total Prize Pool: ₹35,000 – ₹40,000 (excluding games)

Winners across events will receive not just cash prizes but also exclusive internship opportunities from partner industries.

Recognition, networking, and innovation — the true rewards of CHARGE REVAMPED.
