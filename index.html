ABOUT US

The Electronix Club is a community of passionate electronics and technology enthusiasts dedicated to promoting innovation, creativity, and practical learning among students. Through events like Charge Revamped 2026, the club creates a platform where students can explore emerging technologies, collaborate with peers, and transform their ideas into impactful solutions. Our initiatives include technical workshops, hackathons, project exhibitions, and interactive sessions with industry professionals. These activities help students gain hands-on experience and exposure to real-world engineering challenges. The club also focuses on providing opportunities for internships, industry recognition, and collaboration with technology companies. Students get the chance to build strong professional networks, interact with experts, and gain valuable insights into the latest advancements in electronics and technology. By participating in Electronix Club activities, students develop technical skills, leadership abilities, and innovative thinking that prepare them for successful careers in the technology industry.
