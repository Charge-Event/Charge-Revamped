About the Event

CHARGE REVAMPED is where ideas spark, circuits connect, and imagination gets electrified. A celebration of innovation, coding, creativity, and pure engineering brilliance. This fest redefines how technology meets imagination — from electronics to embedded systems and IoT marvels.

Join thousands of visionaries as we combine hardware, software, and design thinking to create the next generation of technology.
