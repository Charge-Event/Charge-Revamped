Welcome to CHARGE 2026

Organized by The Electronics Club(TEC), SNIST
A celebration of innovation in Electronics, Embedded Systems, IoT, VLSI, PCB Design, and Drones.

Experience innovation and technology at CHARGE REVAMPED 2026 — a 3-day national-level technical fest organized by The Electronics Club(TEC), SNIST.

The fest features an exciting lineup of events: Forge (24-hour Hackathon), Workshop on Embedded Systems, Ideathon, Project Expo, and Paper Presentation — plus fun technical and non-technical games.

Prize Pool: ₹35,000 – ₹40,000 (excluding games)
Expected Footfall: 7,000 + students (including participants from other universities)

Build, innovate and compete for internships and recognition at one of the most electrifying technical events of the year!
