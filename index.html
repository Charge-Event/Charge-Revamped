Welcome to CHARGE REVAMPED 2026

Organized by The Electronix Club, CHARGE 2026 is a technology-driven event that brings together passionate innovators, engineers, and students who are eager to explore the world of electronics and modern engineering. Through interactive sessions, competitions, and collaborative learning experiences, CHARGE 2026 promotes innovation and technical excellence. The event focuses on exciting domains such as Electronics System Design, Embedded Systems, IoT, PCB Design, Robotics, and Drone Technology, creating an environment that inspires experimentation and technological advancement.

Events

Hackathon

The Hackathon challenges participants to develop innovative electronics solutions within a limited time. Teams will build smart systems using embedded platforms, sensors, and automation technologies while solving real-world problems.

Workshops

Hands-on workshops introduce participants to technologies such as IoT development, PCB design, embedded programming, and hardware prototyping through guided practical sessions.

Project Expo

Students showcase their electronics innovations including robotics systems, automation solutions, IoT devices, and experimental hardware prototypes to visitors and industry experts.

Paper Presentation

Participants present research papers and emerging technology ideas related to electronics, communication systems, and futuristic hardware innovations.

Ideathon

Teams propose creative technological ideas addressing real-world challenges using electronics and engineering concepts, evaluated based on innovation and feasibility.

Technical Games

Interactive electronics games test participants' logical thinking, circuit knowledge, and engineering creativity in a fun competitive environment.

Prototyping Event

Participants rapidly build working hardware models using electronic components and microcontrollers to demonstrate innovation and engineering creativity.

CHARGE 2026 features a total prize pool of ₹40,000 across multiple competitions.

More than 7000+ visitors are expected to attend the event to explore projects, attend workshops, and experience emerging technologies.
