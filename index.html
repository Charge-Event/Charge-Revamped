About the Event

CHARGE REVAMPED is a celebration of electronics, innovation, and futuristic design. It’s a platform where circuits spark ideas, and ideas ignite revolutions.

Designed for creators, engineers, and dreamers — this fest unites tech enthusiasts from across the nation to explore Embedded Systems, IoT, VLSI, and Drone Technologies.
