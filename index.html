Welcome to CHARGE REVAMPED 2026

Organized by The Electronix Club, CHARGE 2026 is a technology-driven event that brings together passionate innovators, engineers, and students who are eager to explore the world of electronics and modern engineering. Through interactive sessions, competitions, and collaborative learning experiences, CHARGE 2026 aims to promote innovation and technical excellence among students. The event focuses on exciting domains such as Electronics System Design, Embedded Systems, Internet of Things (IoT), PCB Design, Robotics, and Drone Technology.
