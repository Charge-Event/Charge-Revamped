About CHARGE

CHARGE is a celebration of innovation, creativity, and engineering excellence. For over a decade, it has been the platform where students transform ideas into reality — from embedded systems, IoT, and drones to VLSI and PCB design.

CHARGE is not just an event; it is an ecosystem of inspiration where participants collaborate, compete, and innovate. Every year, students across the country bring their ideas to life and showcase projects that redefine technology.
