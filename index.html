About CHARGE

CHARGE is the crown jewel of TEC, Department of ECE, SNIST, a fest that has consistently inspired creativity, experimentation, and excellence. Over the past decade, CHARGE has been the stage for hundreds of projects, ingenious prototypes, and groundbreaking research. Students have showcased innovations from autonomous drones to IoT smart devices, making CHARGE a hub for technical brilliance.

This fest not only celebrates technology but cultivates collaboration, mentorship, and exposure. Participants get a chance to learn from experts, compete against the best, and gain recognition at a national level. Every edition has brought together 7,000+ attendees, including students from SNIST, other universities, and tech enthusiasts from all over the country.

Major Attractions

⚙️ Workshop

Hands-on training with experienced mentors. Participants learn embedded systems, IoT, drones, PCB design, and real-world electronics applications in a live environment.

💡 Forge Hackathon

24-hour nonstop hackathon challenging students to innovate in electronics, IoT, and drone technology. A platform to ideate, design, and implement under pressure.

🚀 Ideathon

Pitch your ideas to industry experts and peers. Collaborate, refine, and transform raw concepts into potential solutions that could impact society and technology.

🧩 Project Expo

Showcase your creations to an audience of 7,000+ attendees, including academicians and industry leaders. Gain feedback, recognition, and opportunities for internships and collaborations.

📑 Paper Presentation

Share your research, innovations, and technical papers with peers and experts. Foster knowledge exchange and explore new frontiers in technology.

🎮 Games

Recharge your energy with fun, interactive games — blending technology, strategy, and entertainment. Energy never rests at CHARGE!
