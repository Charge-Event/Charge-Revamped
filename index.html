About CHARGE

CHARGE is a decade-long legacy of creativity and excellence. Since its inception, it has been the flagship event of TEC, Department of ECE, SNIST, inspiring students to innovate and lead.

For over 10 years, CHARGE has been a platform for brilliant minds to bring electronics to life — from embedded systems to drones, IoT innovations, and VLSI marvels.

Each edition brings fresh challenges, competitions, workshops, and hackathons that electrify imagination and foster collaboration.

CHARGE is not just an event; it’s a movement that transforms ideas into reality and celebrates the spirit of innovation.

Students from SNIST and other universities converge to compete, learn, and network — making CHARGE a melting pot of talent and creativity.

Every edition adds to its legacy, creating unforgettable experiences and a thriving ecosystem for budding engineers.
