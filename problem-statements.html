Problem Statements

Teams participating in the hackathon can either choose from the official problem statements provided by the organizers or work on their own innovative idea through the Open Innovation track.

Official Problem Statements

Participants can select from the curated problem statements prepared by the organizing team. These challenges focus on solving practical technological problems and encourage teams to build impactful solutions using their technical knowledge, creativity, and engineering skills.

Open Innovation

The Open Innovation track allows teams to work on their own original idea instead of selecting a predefined challenge. Participants are encouraged to identify real-world problems and develop innovative technological solutions that can create meaningful impact in society, industry, or everyday life.

Projects may involve hardware systems, embedded electronics, intelligent software platforms, or a combination of both. The focus is on creativity, feasibility, and the ability to transform an idea into a working prototype or functional solution during the hackathon.
