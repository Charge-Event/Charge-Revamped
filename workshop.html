About The Workshop

This technical workshop is designed to provide students with practical exposure to modern electronics technologies used in industry today. Participants will work with real hardware modules, sensors, and development boards while learning concepts such as embedded programming, IoT device connectivity, and professional PCB design. The session will be guided by experienced mentors and industry professionals who will help participants understand how electronic products are designed and developed in real-world environments.

Workshop Domains

🔌 Embedded Systems

Learn microcontroller programming, hardware interfacing, and real-time system development used in modern electronic devices.

🌐 Internet of Things (IoT)

Build connected smart devices using sensors, WiFi modules, and cloud platforms for real-time monitoring and automation.

🖥 PCB Design

Understand how professional circuit boards are designed using industry-standard tools and electronic design workflows.
