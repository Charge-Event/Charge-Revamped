About The Workshop

This technical workshop is designed to provide practical exposure to modern electronics technologies such as Internet of Things (IoT), Embedded Systems, PCB Design, and Robotics.

Participants will experience hands-on training sessions where they will interact with real hardware components, understand system design principles, and learn how modern electronic devices are developed.

The workshop will be guided by experienced mentors who will help participants understand real-world engineering workflows used in industry.

Workshop Domains

🔌 Embedded Systems

Learn microcontroller programming, hardware interfacing, and real-time system applications used in modern electronics devices.

🌐 Internet of Things (IoT)

Build connected smart devices using sensors, communication modules, and cloud platforms.

🖥 PCB Design

Understand how professional circuit boards are designed and manufactured using modern PCB design tools.
