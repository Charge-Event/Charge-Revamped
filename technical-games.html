Technical Games Arena

Experience electronics in the most exciting way through interactive technical games. Test your knowledge, speed, creativity, and practical skills through fun engineering challenges designed by The Electronix Club. The event will be held at Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology.

About The Event

The Technical Games Arena is designed to make electronics learning engaging, interactive, and competitive. Participants will take part in short technical challenges that test practical circuit knowledge, debugging skills, logic thinking, and quick decision making.


Each activity combines fun with learning, allowing students to explore electronics concepts in a hands-on and competitive environment. These games encourage creativity, teamwork, and confidence while helping participants improve their technical understanding.

Technical Challenges

⚡ Rapid Circuit Building

Participants must quickly assemble a working electronic circuit using components like breadboards, LEDs, resistors, batteries, and connecting wires. The challenge tests speed, accuracy, and hands-on circuit building ability.

🧠 Logic Mapping

A grid containing multiple digital logic gates will be shown briefly to participants. After it disappears, they must recreate the exact arrangement of gates from memory, testing concentration and digital electronics knowledge.

🧩 Quiz

The Technical Knowledge Quiz consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs) based on electronics concepts. The quiz will be conducted as an online game — participants must scan the provided link, log in, and attempt the quiz. A total of 50 electronics questions will be given. Each team must consist of two members, and the registration fee is ₹100 per team. Only one team will be selected as the winner and will receive a cash prize. The quiz will be conducted from 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM (1 hour).

💣 Bomb Diffuser

Participants must prevent a simulated bomb from exploding by correctly disengaging the threat by eliminating the wire which triggers the bomb.

What You Will Gain

  • Hands-on exposure to real electronics concepts
  • Practical circuit building and debugging experience
  • Opportunity to compete with other engineering students
  • Improved problem-solving and analytical thinking
  • Networking with fellow innovators and tech enthusiasts

Additional Fun Activities

Apart from the technical challenges, the event will also include a few non-technical fun games to create a lively and enjoyable environment for all participants. These activities ensure that everyone can participate and experience the energetic atmosphere of The Electronix Club events.
