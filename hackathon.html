About The Hackathon

The CHARGE Hackathon is an intense 24-hour innovation challenge designed to encourage creativity, collaboration, and technical problem solving among engineering students.

Participants will work in teams to design, prototype, and present innovative solutions for real-world problems using modern technologies such as IoT, Embedded Systems, Robotics, and Electronics System Design.

Throughout the hackathon, teams will receive mentorship, interact with experts, and compete to build the most impactful and creative solution within the limited time.
